# Lalith Kumar

Firmware • Hardware Validation • Embedded Systems

Engineering at the boundary between **software and silicon**.

My work and interests sit close to the hardware: firmware, digital systems, verification environments, and the practical problem of making complex systems behave reliably.

Most of the code here reflects that focus — small systems, experiments, and digital design projects that explore how computation actually moves through hardware.

---

## Engineering Approach

I tend to think about systems from the bottom up.

Signals become protocols.
Protocols become subsystems.
Subsystems become products.

Understanding each layer — and how failures propagate across them — is where most interesting engineering problems live.

Areas I spend time exploring include:

* Embedded firmware development
* Hardware validation and board bring-up
* Digital logic design and verification
* Microcontroller-based systems
* Low-level debugging and instrumentation
